# Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of the St. Michaels Adult Family Home website, we are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive collaboration environment.

## Our Standards

- Use respectful and inclusive language.
- Be patient, considerate, and open to different viewpoints.
- Give and accept constructive feedback professionally.
- Respect privacy, especially when discussing residents, families, or contact inquiries.
- Keep project discussions focused on improving the website and its operation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or violence
- Personal insults or discriminatory remarks
- Publishing another person's private information without permission
- Disruptive conduct in issues, commits, reviews, or project discussions
- Misuse of resident, family, customer, or contributor information

## Reporting Conduct Issues

Report unacceptable behavior privately to [support@techibytesmedia.com](mailto:support@techibytesmedia.com). Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communications that violate this code. Violations may result in a warning, temporary restriction, removal from the project, or a permanent ban from future participation.
